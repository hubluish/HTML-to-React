Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

This is a simple and fast recipe, with a preparation time of around 10 minutes.

Ingredients

- 2 large eggs
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, or meat
- Fresh herbs for garnish

Instructions

1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
2. Heat butter in a nonstick pan.
3. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook on low heat.
4. Add your choice of fillings before folding the omelette.
5. Cook until thse eggs are set.
6. Serve hot with garnish.
